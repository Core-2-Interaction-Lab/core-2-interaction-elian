THE GARDEN OF FORKING PATH

On labyrinths and simultaneous, branching realities.

BY JORGE LUIS BORGES

Reality is constructed; we forge our own
paths—even when they diverge and fork.
You must find the totality of flowers in this
labyrinthine garden to reveal Borges’s
text, but much like it, you must look around
the page for clues on how to build your reality.
Look around the page to find the garden
of flowers narrating the text.

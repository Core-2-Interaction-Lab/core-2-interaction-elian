NYC is a metropolis characterized by its diverse
and dynamic nature, and data analysis is crucial
for understanding its inner workings. The Bureau
of Fire Investigation provides detailed
information on fire incidents, causes, and trends
over a period of time.

Ranging from 2016–2020, and found on
data.cityofnewyork.us, this information is
valuable for researchers and policymakers
seeking to comprehend factors contributing to
fires, identify high-risk areas, and develop
effective preventive strategies—or for anyone
looking for insight on these devastating events.
This dataset is an essential tool for
comprehending the diverse facets of life in New
York City and enhancing safety measures.
